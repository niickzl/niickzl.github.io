01

SillyHacks Hackathon  @Surge, SFU

Apr 2026
    Context
  • SillyHacks is a 12-hour hackathon hosted by SFU Surge.
  • My team and I were tasked to create a Silly project to celebrate the spirit of April Fools' Day.
    Our Idea — Aura Monitor
  • A browser extension that detects user keystrokes and classifies text aura using a tailored NLQ model.
  • When "negative aura" is detected, a random pool of images and sound effects pops up on screen.
  • Gemini generates a humorous roast of the detected text, spoken by an Anime Girl voice via ElevenLabs.

Mountain Madness Hackathon  @CSSS, SFU

Mar 2026
    Context
  • A 24-hour hackathon by the Computing Science Student Society (CSSS) at SFU.
  • Our team built for the RBC Track: Personal Finance Platforms — an intelligent platform that transforms multi-calendar life into actionable financial insights.
    Our Idea — RBC (Rational Budgeting Calendar)
  • Connects your calendars and bank account to create smart, adaptive daily spending limits.
  • AI-powered budgeting engine: classifies income and spending, splits budgets by event, redistributes unused limits intelligently.
  • Gamification: goal tracking, daily streaks, friend leaderboards, and a rank system based on savings efficiency.
