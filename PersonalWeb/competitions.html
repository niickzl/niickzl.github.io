SillyHacks Hackathon
@Surge, SFU

Date: Apr 2026

    Context:
  • SillyHacks is a 12-hour hackathon hosted by
    SFU Surge.
  • My team and I are tasked to create a Silly
    project, to celebrate the spirit of April Fools' Day.
    Our Idea — Aura Monitor:
  • Use a browser extension to detect user keystrokes,
    then classify if the text has "aura" or not with a tailored NLQ
  • When our algorithm detects "negative aura", a pool of
    images/sound effects will be randomly chosen to pop up
    on the user's screen.
  • Following that, to include some AI content as we are in 2026,
    the extension will also use Gemini to generate a short and
    humorous "roast" based on the detected text, which is then
    spoken by an Anime Girl voice from ElevenLabs.

Mountain Madness Hackathon
@CSSS, SFU

Date: Mar 2026

    Context:
  • Mountain Madness is a 24-hour hackathon hosted by the
    Computing Science Student Society (CSSS) at SFU.
  • Our team built for the RBC Track: Personal Finance Platforms
    theme: designing an intelligent platform that transforms the
    unpredictable nature of modern multi-calendar life into
    actionable financial insights.
    Our Idea — RBC (Rational Budgeting Calendar):
  • Connects your calendars and bank account to create
    smart, adaptive daily spending limits.
  • AI-powered budgeting engine:
    • LLM classifies income, mandatory, and variable spending
    • Splits term budget into dynamic daily limits by events
    • Redistributes unused limits intelligently across the term
  • Gamification to make saving motivating:
    • Goal tracking with progress bars
    • Daily streaks and friend leaderboards
    • Rank system based on savings efficiency & bonuses
